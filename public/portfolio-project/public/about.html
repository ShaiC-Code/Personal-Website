About Me

My name is Shai Crespo, I'm a Computer Science Major attending Suffolk County Community College in New York. I am currently in my last semester and have a current GPA of 3.8. I plan on transfering into Stony Brook and specializing in Artificial Intelligence while learning video game making on the side. Hopefully blending the two together to create a very interesting product, or simply making games that I have always though would be good ideas. I wish to make progressive research or be around for the development of A.I. becoming more humanlike, becoming able to simulate emotions and subconsciousness. This porfolio and projects listed therein are a result of me learning web development over my colleges Computer Science program. My professor has enlightened me of how useful of a skill it is in the field, even if my goal isn't to become a web developer; a skill to have that makes me adaptable. At the moment I am a beginner, and my projects are very modest.
I plan to improve.
